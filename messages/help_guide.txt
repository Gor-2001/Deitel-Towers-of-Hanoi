NAME
	Towers of Hanoi - a command-line puzzle game

SYNOPSIS
	Move all disks to the right tower.

CONTROLS
	To move the cursor between towers:
		'd': Move cursor to the right
		'a': Move cursor to the left
		'u': Pick up a disk
		's': Place a disk
		'q': Quit the game
		'h': Show this help menu
		To validate a move:
		Enter: Validate your position and win if all disks are in the correct order.

EXIT
	Press 'h' to return to the game.
